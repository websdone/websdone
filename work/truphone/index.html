We've built a different kind of mobile network

When mobile roaming was first introduced, calls from abroad had to be routed via your home country. That meant poor call quality, lag and overall a pretty frustrating experience. Today, it’s exactly the same. Businesses still don’t get the performance, service or pricing they need to compete effectively or internationally.

The Truphone network is different. Traditional operators have a core network in one country, but ours is distributed to deliver better service for businesses around the world. Find out even more about our unique network
